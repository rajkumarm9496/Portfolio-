Design Thinking

How I solve hard problems
in FinTech UX

Challenge

12+ mandate states ‚Äî how to show without overwhelming ops teams?

Grouped into two mental models: User Journey Insight vs Mandate Status. Color-coded cards enable instant at-a-glance scanning for teams reviewing 100s of mandates daily.

Design Decision

4 auth modes √ó 5 banks = 20 different flows in one form

Progressive disclosure form ‚Äî Step 1 reveals relevant bank/auth fields, Step 2 adapts dynamically. Users see only what's relevant, reducing cognitive load and input errors.

Challenge

MSMEs submitting CC loan stock statements via manual Excel sheets

Designed structured digital submission flows with auto-calculation of Drawing Power, alerts for overdue submissions, and ERP-integrated upload ‚Äî eliminating manual errors entirely.

Design Decision

ERP to Bank payments without complex API/H2H integration

Designed the Vanghee 2.0 B2B NPCI Connector UX ‚Äî a plug-and-play interface that allows any ERP to initiate NPCI payments without core architectural changes.

Challenge

Multi-tenant B2B platform ‚Äî data isolation across corporate clients

Prominent dual context switcher (Transaction Type + Corporation) always visible in header ‚Äî scopes every action clearly, building operational confidence and preventing multi-tenant errors.

Design Decision

AI-assisted design-to-dev handoff for faster shipping

Used prompt-driven workflows (Claude + MAKE) to generate dev-ready HTML/CSS/React specs directly from Figma designs ‚Äî reducing handoff time by 40% and front-end rework significantly.
